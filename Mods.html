Sp0ckrates Mods

For almost 5 years now my father and I have been making mods for Skyrim and Fallout. We now have 76 mods and over a million subscribers on steam. Since we have moved over to bethesda we have had even more traction and most of our mods are now available on the XB1 and the PS4.

We are both very passionate about creating awesome and diverse mods that will enhance the gaming experience. We also work hard to make sure that our mods are immersive and fit the lore of the games we are modding. Check out a few of our mods on the right or go right to our modders pages and search through all of our mods.
